The Meaning
Behind LEA

In the Sidama language — Sidaamu Afoo — "Lea" carries a profound meaning: it describes the sacred moment when the coffee bean becomes fully matured. This is the transformation point, when raw potential becomes realized excellence.

From the lush highlands of Sidama, Ethiopia — the birthplace of coffee — every bean tells a story of patience, tradition, and perfection.

Roasted LEA Coffee embodies this philosophy. We source only the finest matured cherries from Ethiopian smallholder farmers, nurturing each batch through a meticulous roasting process that honors centuries of tradition while embracing modern craft.

The Journey

From Cherry to Cup

01

Highland Harvest

At elevations above 2,000 meters in the Sidama highlands, our partner farmers hand-pick only the ripest coffee cherries — the ones that have reached the "Lea" stage of full maturation.

02

Washed Processing

Cherries are meticulously washed and sorted, then sun-dried on traditional raised beds. This brings out the clean, bright flavor profiles that Ethiopian coffees are celebrated for.

03

Artisan Roasting

Each batch is roasted in small quantities by our master roasters, who calibrate temperature and timing to unlock the unique character hidden within every bean.

04

Your Perfect Cup

Freshly packaged and delivered to preserve peak flavor. Every sip connects you to the rich soil, the patient farmer, and the ancient tradition of Ethiopian coffee culture.
