Our Story

Crafted With
Passion & Care

Lea Coffee Roasted brings you the finest Ethiopian beans, sourced directly from family farms in Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, and Harrar. Each bean is hand-picked and carefully selected for quality.

Our slow-roasting process preserves the unique floral, fruity, and chocolatey notes that make our coffee truly exceptional — delivered fresh to your cup.
